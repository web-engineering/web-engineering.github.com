About this Book

This is a textbook for the second year of a computer science or "media informatics" degree program. It builds on Web Development (which is only available in german)

This book was written and published by Brigitte Jellinek in the hope that it will be useful to lecturers and students in educational institutions as well as to self-directed learners on the internet.

This textbook is also available for Kindle.
This whole book is currently a work in progress. While still useful, it may contain incomplete information and even errors. You can help by reviewing them and posting your comments and corrections.
